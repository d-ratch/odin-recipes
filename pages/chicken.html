How to make baked chicken

What's better than a quick, easy, nutritious meal after a long day of work?


Sleep is the only valid answer, but you can't go to bed hungry!

With this recipe I'll show you how to make the world's best meal with

little effort and little time. All you need to bring is the ability to set

the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit

Are you ready?!

You will need:

  • An oven
  • A cooking sheet
  • Tin Foil

  • A meat thermometer
  • 30 minutes
