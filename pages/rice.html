You will need:

  • Rice
  • Water
  • A rice cooker
  • Kikoman Low-Sodium Soy Sauce

Directions:

  1. Place one cup of rice in your rice cooker
  2. CLEAN YOUR F*CKING RICE -> see steps three through five
  3. place enough water in the rice bowl to cover the rice
  4. shake or stir with your fingers until the water turns white
  5. Drain the water and repeat from step three until the water stays clear after agitation
  6. Drain the water one last time
  7. Place one and a half cups of water in rice bowl
  8. Turn on your rice cooker for 30 minutes
  9. after thirty minutes place your rice on a dinner plate and drench that bed in soy sauce!
  10. Bone-apple-tea!
